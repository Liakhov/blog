## A few words

I've been building web apps for over 8 years — from crafting responsive interfaces with **Angular** and **SvelteKit** to designing backend APIs with **Node.js** and **Prisma**. Clean code, solid architecture, and great developer experience — that's what drives me.

My day-to-day is all about **TypeScript** — on the frontend and the backend. I enjoy the kind of work where you take a messy problem and turn it into something that just works.

## Tech stack

- **Frontend:** Angular, SvelteKit, TypeScript, HTML, CSS/SCSS
- **Backend:** Node.js, Prisma
- **Tools:** Git, CI/CD, REST APIs
